What We Missed & Where to Stay Next Time
Places We Want to Hit Next Time
Chapultepec Castle
Hilltop castle with panoramic city views
Culture
⏰ Tue–Sun 9am–5pm 💰 $210 MXN
We went to the park but spent all our time at Antropología. Next time: arrive early, do the castle first, then take the Cablebús.
Cablebús Línea 3 — Aerial Gondola
Cable car over Chapultepec Park · 5.5 km · $7 MXN
Culture
Aerial views over all 4 sections of the park. On a clear day you can see Popocatépetl. Board at Constituyentes station.
Museo de Arte Moderno
Modern art in Chapultepec Park
Culture
⏰ 10:30am–5:30pm 💰 $70 MXN
Right in the park — pair with castle & Cablebús for a full Chapultepec day.
Zócalo & Templo Mayor
Main square + Aztec ruins · Heart of CDMX
Culture
💰 Templo Mayor free on Sundays
The heart of Centro Histórico. Templo Mayor ruins right next to the cathedral. Free on Sundays. Pair with La Opera cantina nearby.
La Opera
Historic cantina · Est. 1870s · Centro
Food
5 de Mayo 10, Centro · Maps ↗
Beautiful 19th-century interior. Go for lunch or drinks after Zócalo. There's a bullet hole in the ceiling from Pancho Villa.
Masala y Maíz
Indian-Mexican fusion · One Michelin star
Food
⏰ Noon–6pm only 📍 Marsella 72, Juárez
Wasn't open during our visit. One Michelin star — reservations essential. Communal table, shared dishes.
Ātma Yoga — Power Vinyasa
English class · Roma Norte
Wellness
📍 Yucatán 69, 2nd Floor 💰 $300 MXN
Melissa did yoga at home instead — but next time, the English Power Vinyasa class is the move. Book via Mindbody.

Where to Stay Next Time
Condesa
Tree-lined streets · Art Deco · Parks
We kept walking to Condesa — we loved the Amsterdam loop, the tree canopy, and the cafe culture. Next time, stay right in it. Look for an Airbnb near Parque México or along Amsterdam. Walkable to Roma, near Chapultepec, and the restaurant scene is incredible.
Coyoacán
Bohemian village · Frida Kahlo · Markets
Totally different vibe — quieter, more residential, colorful markets, cobblestone streets. Frida Kahlo Museum is here. Would give a whole new perspective on the city.
Lancaster 31 (Again!)
Col. Juárez · 5th floor · Depto 501
Honestly? The apartment was great. Walking distance to everything in Roma and Juárez. The balcony was clutch. Cards with the doors open. Could easily stay here again and just explore further out each day.
